Who Can Use Get Memberly?


Get Memberly is for businesses and individuals who struggle with how to maximize the ROI of association and group memberships.

Are you getting the most out of your memberships in groups and organizations? Do you know which groups best generate leads and referrals for your business? Most people join a group or association for one reason or another, but never fully capitalize on that membership.

Are you leaving money on the table and ignoring opportunities for growth? Many people do, and in today's world, maximizing every opportunity has become a lot more important.

About

Associations and groups are my passion. For over 20 years, I have been working for not-for-profits, non-profits, even a government agency thrown into the mix (with time out in the corporate world for some interesting balance and perspective) and know well the value they provide. But far too often, that value is not fully tapped. Money gets left on the table and opportunities are wasted. Members don't understand what benefits they have or even how to take full advantage of the ones they are familiar with. Associations and groups grapple with ways to better serve their members, but fall short. And something gets lost in translation.

Get Memberly helps companies and executives connect the dots ... to more opportunities and better, faster results. We help unlock the potential that is there for the taking ... if you know how.

- building on-line and off-line communities

- noun
