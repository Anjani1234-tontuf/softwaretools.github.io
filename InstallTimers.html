MTimerApp.html is an html page turned application.
Set up to 6 of each, Count Down, Stop Watch and at a specific Time timers.
The screen size it s set to width 320px and a height of 356px, a nice size for smartphones.

At a Time, CountDown,CountUp, StopWatch Timers, Kitchen, Event, To Do Timers.

The Button at the top with the inital value of "Count Down Timers" is a toggle button that takes you
to the other Timer functions
Click on the button, The Stop Watch screen appears.
Click on the button again the Time alarms screen appears

The stopwatch screen has the 2 toggle buttons, to "Display options" and to "Display Help"
Help is the documentation for this application.
When you click on either of these 2 toggle buttons, they switch from ..Display to ..Hide


These timers are not accurate enough for scientific or competitive sports.
They are perfect for nap taking, cooking and other events where microsecond accuracy is not required.
